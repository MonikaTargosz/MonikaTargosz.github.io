About me

Few words about me...

Monika Targosz

Programmer

I am a graduate of Automation and Robotics at the Faculty of Automatics, Electronics and Computer Science at the Silesian University of Technology. The specialization of the master's studies concerned measurement and information systems. My interests in the field of automation are, in particular, embedded systems, DSP and system control. In my spare time I like to be outdoors, especially in the Polish mountains.
